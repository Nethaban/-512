How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one, keeping their value!

Note: This game utilises the classic 10s (plus 22). These are 31, 32, 42, 69, 89, 95, 100, 106, 121, 142 and 150.

Based on 1024 by Veewo Studio and Threes by Asher Vollmer.
